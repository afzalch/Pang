WELCOME TO OPENGL PANG!
=============================================================================

Use the A and D keys to move Emma left and right respectively.

Press the spacebar to shoot the harpoon! A bubble will split into two smaller 
bubbles until it reaches its smallest size.

Emma loses health every time a bubble touches her, if her health drops down to 
0, GAME OVER.

If all of the bubbles are not popped before the timer hits 0, game over.

Right click to select difficulty level.

To restart the game, just press R key.

To exit, press q or esc.

=============================================================================

textures (background and character's are made of textures)
